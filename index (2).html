A Universe Beyond Imagination

Marvel Entertainment, originally founded as Timely Comics in 1939 by Martin Goodman, has evolved into one of the most influential entertainment conglomerates in global history. From its humble beginnings in the Golden Age of comics, Marvel introduced the world to flawed, relatable heroes who transcended the boundaries of fiction.

Under the visionary leadership of Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, and Steve Ditko, Marvel revolutionized storytelling by imbuing its characters with genuine human struggles, moral complexities, and emotional depth. Today, the Marvel Cinematic Universe stands as the highest-grossing film franchise of all time, a testament to the enduring power of heroic narratives.
